• How to play
  • Multiplayer
You can move in four directions accross the field - up, down, left and right. You can pick up and move figures. Your goal is to move every figure while walking the shortest possible path. If you walk too much over it, you Nerva is rising. When the Nerva is 100, you're too angry to continue and the game is over.

To move upwards, press the up arrow key (↑) on your keyboard.

To move downwards, press the down arrow key (↓) on your keyboard.

To move left, press the left arrow key (←) on your keyabord.

To move right, press the right arrow key (→) on your keyabord.

To pick up or put down a figure, press the V key on (V) on your keyboard.
